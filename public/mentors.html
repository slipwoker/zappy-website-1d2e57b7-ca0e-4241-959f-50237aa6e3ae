How mentorship works.

Our structured mentorship program for students guides you from goals to mastery in four clear steps. Each phase is designed to fit your schedule and build real-world HubSpot skills with personalized support.

Quick goals intake

We start with a brief conversation to understand your current experience, career goals, and timeline. This helps us tailor the roadmap to your specific needs—no lengthy forms, just a focused discussion.

Personalized roadmap

Your mentor creates a step-by-step learning plan aligned with your goals. You'll see exactly what to learn, when to learn it, and how each skill connects to real HubSpot projects.

Weekly practice sessions

Dedicate focused time each week to hands-on exercises and live mentor sessions. Practice real scenarios, ask questions, and build confidence with immediate feedback—all at a pace that fits your schedule.

Review and next plan

At the end of each cycle, we review your progress, celebrate wins, and adjust the plan as needed. You'll always know what's next and how far you've come on your HubSpot journey.
